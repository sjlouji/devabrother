Welcome to Brotherhood

“This is the work of God;
it is a surprise to our eyes”

Brotherhood Matriculation Higher Secondary School, first known as Brotherhood English School, had its humble beginning in the year 1991 with a few students from LKG to V standard in a rented house in Barathiyar saalai, in Palavakkam.

The opening permission was obtained in the year 1996-1997 for Standard 1 to 7. The temporary recognition was granted in the year 1997-1998 to 1999 - 2000 and the school was upgraded up to Standard 10. Again the school was upgraded to Higher Secondary level from 2001-2002. Our school is one of the rarest of the rarest with 30 years of history in ECR with name and fame. The reason for this, is the performance of the school in the Board Examinations, Sports and Games, Extra and Co-curricular activities right from the beginning.
